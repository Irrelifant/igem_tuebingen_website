Team iGEM TÃ¼bingen 2012/13 has developed a yeast-based sensor for progestin, a pollutant in wastewater which causes hormonal disbalance in fish. If progestin is detected within a sample, luciferin expression is induced in yeast cells via the activation of the luciferin promoter through a G-protein coupled cascade. Accordingly, the presence of progestin is determined and may be analysed quantitatively via a spectrometer.
